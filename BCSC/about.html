About BCSC

The premier cybersecurity championship in the Balkans, bringing together the brightest minds in information security.

Our Mission

BCSC aims to foster cybersecurity talent in the Balkan region by providing a competitive platform for skills development, knowledge sharing, and professional networking. We believe in creating opportunities for the next generation of cybersecurity experts.

Vision

To become the leading cybersecurity competition in Eastern Europe, setting the standard for technical excellence and professional development in the field.

Values

  • • Technical Excellence
  • • Fair Competition
  • • Innovation
  • • Community Building
