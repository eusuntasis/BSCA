Competition Format

Test your skills against the best cybersecurity teams in the Balkans through multiple challenging rounds.

Online Qualifiers

A 24-hour online qualification round where teams compete remotely to solve various challenges.

  • • Duration: 24 hours
  • • Top 20 teams qualify for semifinals
  • • Multiple challenge categories
  • • Remote participation

Semifinals

An intense 36-hour competition featuring advanced challenges and real-time attack/defense scenarios.

  • • Duration: 36 hours
  • • Top 8 teams advance to finals
  • • Advanced challenge categories
  • • Hybrid format (online + onsite)

Grand Finals

The ultimate 48-hour onsite competition to determine the Balkan cybersecurity champions.

  • • Duration: 48 hours
  • • 8 finalist teams
  • • Expert-level challenges
  • • Live onsite event

Challenge Categories

Web Security

Application security, API vulnerabilities, and web exploitation challenges.

Reverse Engineering

Binary analysis, malware investigation, and software exploitation.

Forensics

Digital forensics, memory analysis, and network traffic investigation.

Network Security

Network protocols, traffic analysis, and infrastructure security.

Cryptography

Encryption challenges, cipher analysis, and cryptographic attacks.
